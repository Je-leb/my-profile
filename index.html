A bit of history...

Porsche is a German car manufacturer. Founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the brand has kept the same spirit for all these years.

A model that stands out from the crowd, the 911.

The first generation was marketed in 1964. The engine architecture has remained unchanged until today. It is a flat-six cylinder, arranged in a rear cantilevered position. It is the star of the German brand..
